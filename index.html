Malaysia Viral News

Berita viral Malaysia dalam format video pendek — automated daily.

What it does

Malaysia Viral News is an automated content pipeline that monitors Malaysian news outlets and trending social media every day, rewrites each story in a punchy short-form style, generates a vertical video with voiceover, and publishes the result to the operator's own social media channels.

How it works

1. Scrape

RSS feeds from Berita Harian, Astro Awani, BBC, FMT, Reddit r/malaysia, and Google Trends MY.

2. Rewrite

An AI rewriter turns each long article into a 15-second voice script and a punchy headline.

3. Generate

FFmpeg builds a 1080×1920 vertical video with TTS voiceover, captions, and background music.

4. Publish

Each video is uploaded automatically to the operator's TikTok inbox and posted to X.
