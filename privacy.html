Privacy Policy

Malaysia Viral News — Last updated: 2026-04-23

1. Introduction

Malaysia Viral News ("we", "us", "the Service") is a news and viral content aggregation platform operated from Malaysia. We publish short-form video summaries of publicly reported news to our own TikTok, Facebook, Threads, and web channels, and we allow authorised operators (currently only the platform owner) to connect their TikTok account to the Service for the purpose of publishing those videos on their behalf. This Privacy Policy explains what information we process, why we process it, and how it is protected.

2. What Information We Collect

2.1 Information from TikTok

When an operator connects a TikTok account to the Service through TikTok's OAuth consent screen, TikTok shares the following with us, with the operator's explicit approval:

  • Basic account information (via the user.info.basic scope) — TikTok Open ID, display name, and avatar URL. Used only to show the operator which account is currently connected.
  • Video upload capability (via video.upload) — used to upload video files the operator has generated through the Service to the operator's own TikTok drafts.
  • Video publish capability (via video.publish) — used to post those videos directly to the operator's TikTok profile when the operator triggers a publish action.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens — short-lived tokens issued by TikTok so the Service can call the TikTok Content Posting API on the operator's behalf without storing the operator's TikTok password. We never see or store the operator's TikTok password.

We do not request or access TikTok direct messages, follower lists, comments, analytics, or the private video libraries of any other TikTok user.

2.2 Information from Website Visitors

The public website (malaysiaviralnews) collects standard server logs (timestamp, IP address, user-agent) as part of hosting. We do not use tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or cross-site identifiers. If Google AdSense or affiliate widgets are embedded on article pages, those third-party scripts may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate the operator's TikTok account and keep the session alive.
  • To upload and publish videos the operator has explicitly generated via the Service.
  • To display the operator's connected account name inside the Service's admin view.
  • To refresh OAuth tokens automatically so the operator does not have to re-authorise every day.

We do not sell, rent, or share TikTok-derived data with any third party. We do not use TikTok data to train machine-learning models. We do not combine TikTok data with data from other services.

4. How We Store and Protect Information

OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally in a .env file on the operator's server, outside of any public directory and outside of version control (the .env file is explicitly listed in .gitignore). The server is only reachable by the operator. Tokens are transmitted only over HTTPS to TikTok's official endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com).

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens remain valid until the operator revokes them or until TikTok invalidates them. When the operator disconnects their TikTok account (see Section 7), the corresponding tokens are deleted from our .env file within the same session. We retain no TikTok-derived data after disconnection.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share TikTok data with third parties. Media files the operator chooses to upload are transmitted only to TikTok. The Service does not route TikTok data through any analytics, advertising, or marketing vendor.

7. Your Rights — Revoking Access

The operator may revoke the Service's access to their TikTok account at any time using either of the following methods:

  • Via TikTok: open the TikTok app → Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions and remove "Malaysia Viral News." TikTok will invalidate the associated tokens immediately.
  • Via this Service: delete the TIKTOK_ACCESS_TOKEN and TIKTOK_REFRESH_TOKEN entries from the .env file and restart the Service. The Service will stop calling TikTok on the operator's behalf on the next cycle.

The operator may also request complete deletion of all Service-side TikTok data by emailing the address below; we will confirm deletion within 7 days.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be announced on the Service's home page.
