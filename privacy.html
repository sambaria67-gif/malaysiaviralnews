Privacy Policy

Malaysia Viral News (AutoMalaysia) — Last updated: 2026-04-11

1. Introduction

Malaysia Viral News ("the Service") is an automated content aggregator operated for personal, non-commercial use. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Service processes and how it is handled.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Service does not have user accounts, login forms, comment sections, or any mechanism by which visitors can submit personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses
  • Payment information
  • Login credentials
  • Direct messages or private content

3. Information Processed Automatically

When social media platforms (TikTok, X, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram) deliver the Service's published content to viewers, those platforms may collect standard analytics such as view counts and engagement metrics. This data is governed by the respective platform's own privacy policy, not this one.

4. Source Content

The Service ingests publicly available news articles and social media posts. No private or password-protected content is accessed. Source authors and outlets are credited or linked where appropriate.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party APIs to publish generated videos:

  • TikTok Content Posting API — to upload video drafts to the operator's own TikTok account
  • X (Twitter) API — to publish posts to the operator's own X account

These services receive only the operator's own access tokens and the video files being published. No third-party user data is sent.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Service does not set cookies, use tracking pixels, or run analytics scripts on its public pages.

7. Data Retention

Generated videos and metadata are stored locally on the operator's own machine and are not shared with any third party except the publishing APIs listed above.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly process data from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the operator through the social media channels operated under the AutoMalaysia / Malaysia Viral News brand.
